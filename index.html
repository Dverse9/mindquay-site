The docking ritual

Dump every circling thought

Open MindQuay in the evening and write down whatever is still looping — big or small. If it is in your head, it goes on the list.

Sort each one

For every thought, choose: let it go, save it for tomorrow, or flag it as something that needs action. Each decision takes seconds.

Wind down with sound and breath

Finish with a generated harbor soundscape and a guided round of 4-7-8 breathing, and let the day end where it belongs.

Wake to a clear deck

In the morning, only the thoughts you chose to carry are waiting — parked items and flagged actions, ready when you are.
