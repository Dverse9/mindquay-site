Frequently asked

Do I have to pay for MindQuay?

No. MindQuay is free, and every feature is available to everyone at no cost — the docking ritual, triage, breathing pacer, the three harbor soundscapes, the weekly review, and reminders. If it’s helped and you’d like to support it, you can leave an optional tithe (a one-time gift, or a little each month). A tithe is never required and unlocks nothing.

What does a monthly tithe include?

A monthly tithe supports ongoing development, and as a small thank-you it adds two extra procedurally generated soundscapes. That’s the only difference — every core feature and the original three soundscapes stay free for everyone, always.

How do I cancel a monthly tithe?

Monthly tithes are managed by Apple, not by MindQuay, so you cancel through iOS:

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
  • Tap your name at the top (Apple Account).
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Tap MindQuay, then tap Cancel Subscription.

The extra soundscapes stay available until the end of the period you’ve already paid for. Because the app is free, the rest of MindQuay is unaffected. One-time tithes are single charges and have nothing to cancel.

How do I restore a tithe on a new phone?

Only a monthly tithe needs restoring (one-time tithes are single gifts with nothing to re-enable). Sign in with the same Apple Account you tithed with, then open MindQuay and go to Settings > Restore purchases. Your supporter soundscapes will be recognized right away.

Where is my data stored?

On your device, and only on your device. MindQuay stores everything locally using Apple's SwiftData. There is no account, no server, and no sync. Your thoughts never leave your phone.

How do I delete my data?

Open MindQuay and go to Settings > Delete my thoughts & history. All your thoughts and history are erased instantly. Because all data lives on your device, deleting the app itself removes everything; there is no copy anywhere else.

Why don't the soundscapes ever repeat?

Harbor Waves, Quiet Rain, and Still Water aren't recordings — they are synthesized live on your device, moment by moment. Because the sound is generated procedurally rather than played from a file, it never loops and no two sessions ever sound quite the same.

Does MindQuay work offline?

Yes. The docking ritual, soundscapes, breathing pacer, and weekly review all work completely offline, because nothing depends on a server. The only thing that needs an internet connection is leaving or restoring a tithe, which goes through Apple.

Is MindQuay a therapy or medical app?

No. MindQuay is a self-care tool for winding down and clearing your head. It is not medical care, therapy, or a crisis service, and it is not a substitute for professional help. If you're struggling, please reach out to a qualified professional. If you're in crisis in the US, call or text 988 to reach the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.

Refunds

All tithes are processed by Apple, so refunds are handled by Apple as well; MindQuay can't issue them directly. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple Account, and choose the MindQuay tithe. If you run into trouble, email us at support@mindquay.com and we'll point you in the right direction.
